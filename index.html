Welcome to The Magnus Café

Our coffee has been the fuel for the researchers and other patrons alike for over 200 years! Magnus Café was established in 1820, 2 years after the founding of the institue beside which we reside.

Since the time of our founding we have worked tirelessly in perfecting our recipies and atmosphere, changing with the times, everyone to enjoy and have a small respite from the chaos in their lives and outside of them.
